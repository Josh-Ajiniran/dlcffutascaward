Welfare

We do not pay lip service to love, while our actions speak to the contrary. We attend to the present needs of members and ensure their rounded wholeness as we see each member as one part of the whole body of Christ. Thus, endeavouring to keep the unity of faith and fellowship.

What we believe

As a fellowship, we are avowed believers in the totality of completeness of the Bible comprising 39 books of the Old Testament and 27 books of the new Testament. With no strings attached, we take the Bible at face value and adopt its answers as the final authority on all matters of life and living. It remains our Magna Carta, Guidebook in the wilderness of life and code for living. We study it diligently and systematically with an eye to glean truths that will secure our earthly fulfillment and eternal bliss. Are you a sincere seeker of GOD and His truths? Get along with us as we enjoy a blissful Fellowship with the Living Word (Jesus Christ) and reveive an in-depth revelation of the written word.
